UDOIT Accessibility Scanner

Enhance online learning by removing accessibility barriers from online course content.

What Is UDOIT?

UDOIT is a free and open-source tool that teachers and institutions can use to find and repair common accessibility issues in their online course content. It integrates directly with the Canvas Learning Management System using the LTI standard, meaning that faculty and administrators can access it directly through Canvas without needing to leave the platform or log in separately.
